Bright Light by Saint Clair

  • Black currant
  • Green capsicum
  • Apple blossom

  • Pairings

  • Seafood

Nestled in the pristine landscapes of Marlborough, New Zealand, Saint Clair Family Estate Winery stands as a beacon of excellence in the world of winemaking. Established in 1994 by Neal and Judy Ibbotson, Saint Clair has grown from a small, family-owned boutique winery into one of New Zealand's most acclaimed wine producers. With a commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, the winery has garnered international recognition for its exceptional wines that showcase the unique terroir of Marlborough.

Marlborough, with its unique combination of climate, soils, and sunlight, provides an ideal environment for grape cultivation. Saint Clair's vineyards, spanning the Wairau and Awatere Valleys, benefit from this diversity, allowing the winery to produce a wide range of varietals that showcase the best of New Zealand's wine potential.

Saint Clair Family Estate Winery's story is one of passion, dedication, and a deep connection to the land. The Ibbotson family's commitment to sustainable and environmentally friendly practices is evident in every aspect of the winemaking process. From vineyard management to winery operations, Saint Clair prioritizes practices that respect the delicate balance of nature.

The winery's portfolio is a testament to the diversity and versatility of Marlborough's terroir. From crisp Sauvignon Blancs to elegant Pinot Noirs and complex Chardonnays, each wine reflects the meticulous attention to detail and craftsmanship that defines Saint Clair. One standout offering in their lineup is the Saint Clair Vicar’s Choice Bright Light Sauvignon Blanc, a wine that captures the essence of Marlborough's signature varietal.

As the bottle is uncorked, the Bright Light Sauvignon Blanc greets the senses with an explosion of citrus and tropical fruit notes. The aroma is a medley of zesty grapefruit, passionfruit, and hints of fresh-cut grass, creating an olfactory experience that foreshadows the lively palate to come. On tasting, the wine reveals a perfect balance of acidity, offering crispness and a refreshing quality that is emblematic of the Marlborough style.

What sets the Saint Clair Vicar’s Choice Bright Light Sauvignon Blanc apart is not only its vibrant character but also the meticulous winemaking process that goes into its creation. The grapes are harvested at optimal ripeness to ensure a spectrum of flavors, and the winemaking team employs modern techniques to preserve the freshness and purity of the fruit. The result is a Sauvignon Blanc that encapsulates the essence of Marlborough in every sip.

This wine has become a symbol of Saint Clair's unwavering commitment to quality, and its inclusion in the Vicar’s Choice range underscores the winery's dedication to offering wines that are not only approachable but also showcase the distinctiveness of the Marlborough region.

Saint Clair Family Estate Winery invites wine enthusiasts to embark on a sensory journey through their portfolio, where each bottle tells a story of the land, the climate, and the passion of those who cultivate the vines. The Saint Clair Vicar’s Choice Bright Light Sauvignon Blanc, with its radiant personality and Marlborough charm, exemplifies the excellence that has become synonymous with this esteemed New Zealand winery.
